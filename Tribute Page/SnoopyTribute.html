Brief time line of the Peanuts character Snoopy:

  • 1950 - Snoopy appeared on October 4, two days after the first Peanuts strip. He was one of the four original characters, along with Charlie Brown, Patty, and Shermy.
  • 1952 - On March 16, his thoughts were first shown in a thought balloon.
  • 1956 - He first appeared upright on his hind legs on January 9, when he was shown sliding across a sheet of ice after Shermy and Lucy had first done so.
  • 1958 - He is first shown sleeping on top of his doghouse rather than inside it on December 12.
  • 1965 - First time he adopts his World War I Flying Ace persona, on October 10.
  • 2000 - Snoopy's final appearance in the comic was on February 13, when he was shown sitting on top of his doghouse typing Schulz's farewell message to his readers.
